What is "Tom & Jerry"?

The American animated comedy called "Tom and Jerry" follows a foolish cat's never-ending hunt for a cunning mouse. Jerry is the clever mouse, while Tom is the cunning cat. The characters hardly ever spoke; the action and visual humor carried the entire series.


Some popular characters

Tom

Tom is the scheming cat that chases Jerry.

Jerry

Jerry is the cunning mouse that escapes Tom and overcomes his plans.

Spike

Spike is the main enemy in the series and chases Tom and Jerry on sight.
